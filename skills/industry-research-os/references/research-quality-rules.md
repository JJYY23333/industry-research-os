# Research Quality Rules

## Do

- Build structure before writing conclusions.
- Separate player, model, chain, metric, user, trend, and opportunity documents.
- Use recent sources for current market facts.
- Quote only short excerpts when needed; prefer summaries.
- Store links in `sources.md`.
- Mark uncertainty explicitly.
- Add "待验证问题" when a claim is useful but not fully supported.

## Avoid

- Do not write only a long report and leave no reusable database.
- Do not hide uncertainty.
- Do not mix source grades.
- Do not compare companies without normalizing mode, region, and time range.
- Do not treat media speculation as confirmed facts.
- Do not use stale data for current market structure when recent sources are available.

## Good Industry Questions

- Who are the important players and what modes do they represent?
- Where does gross profit come from?
- Which costs prevent scale?
- Which part of the supply chain controls the bottleneck?
- What changed in the last 12-24 months?
- Which opportunities are supported by observable unmet demand?
- What must be verified before acting?
